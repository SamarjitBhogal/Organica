The Problem

Costs of living are at an all time high and students are struggling to afford to feed themselves, having to resort to BCIT's food bank program.

Our Solution

Through our app, we aim to mitigate this burden by establishing a direct link between consumers and local farmers. By cutting out intermediaries, we offer cost-effective alternatives to conventional grocery shopping.

Hollistic Wellness

According to the Provincial Health Services Authority, Farm-to-school initiatives have the potential to contribute to healthy eating. This is crucial for a healthier body and mind in an increasingly isolated and challenging world.

Community Engagement

The availability of culturally appropriate/traditional fresh fruits and vegetables can be an important part of healthy eating for immigrant populations.
